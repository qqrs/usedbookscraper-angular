about


usedbookscraper was born for two reasons:

  1. a long list of books I wanted to read
  2. the condition of broke-ness that tends to accompany encounters with institutions of higher education

(a contributing factor was the deliciousness of used books in general — the smells, the marginalia from bookworms and distressed students, the business cards and horse track picks and plane tickets as bookmarks, et cetera)
