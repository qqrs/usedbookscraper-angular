about


usedbookscraper is an all-polished-up version of a script I created way back when I first discovered that shipping is cheap on half.com if you order multiple books from the same seller.

I love used books — they smell nice and sometimes they come with imagination-provoking hints about past readers. Notes in the margins. Heartfelt inscriptions. Plane ticket bookmarks. Horse track picks. Cutouts of celebrities from mid‑90s tabloids. Makes you wonder...
