about


usedbookscraper was born when I discovered that half.com has cheap shipping if you order multiple books from the same seller.

At the time I had accumulated a far-too-long list of books I wanted to read, and was too broke to be buying piles of new books due to the side effects of higher education.

So here it is, a couple years later, all polished up.
