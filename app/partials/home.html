how it works:
  1. get your reading list from goodreads.com or enter isbns directly
  2. select 5 – 25 books for the search
  3. tweak options to prioritize certain books, pick specific editions, and set max price and minimum acceptable book condition
  4. usedbookscraper searches half.com and shows you results grouped by seller
  5. buy a stack of books from one seller and get cheap shipping
